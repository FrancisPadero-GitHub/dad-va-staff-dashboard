The Three Definitions
✓
GROSS = the full amount on the customer's invoice
Before any deductions. Not the tech's share. Not the company share. Not the net. If the customer paid $1,799, gross = $1,799.
Definition 1
✓
NET AFTER PARTS = Gross minus the cost of materials used
If no parts → Net After Parts = Gross. If parts were used → subtract them first. Example: $1,799 gross − $50 parts = $1,749 net after parts.
Definition 2
✓
COMPANY NET = Net After Parts × the company's percentage
The split always applies to Net After Parts, not Gross. Example (50%): $1,749 × 50% = $874.50. Example (75% co): $396 × 75% = $297. Example (60% co): $2,000 × 60% = $1,200.
Definition 3
Step-By-Step Formula
How to calculate company net for any job
